about us

此网站旨在去除刻板印象，帮助科普各民族多样文化。通过展现各民族的丰富多彩的文化和解析偏见产生的原因，来减少人们因为不了解一个文化而产生的偏见。

This website aims to eliminate stereotypes and promote public awareness of the diverse cultures among various ethnic groups. By showcasing the rich and varied cultures of different ethnicities and analyzing the root causes of biases, it seeks to reduce prejudice stemming from a lack of understanding of other cultures.
