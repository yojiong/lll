They reflect the local characteristics. Different cultures have developed through integration, been passed down during development, and innovated in the process of inheritance, thus forming the current diversified cultural landscape of mutual enrichment and harmony in the world

总结Conclusion

全世界展现出精彩绝伦的文化盛景，每种文化都独具特色，给人深刻 的印象。不同的文化相互交融，交流，丰富了文化的形式与内涵，给 予人们精神滋养。我们当用包容，自由的心怀，去对待每一种文化， 推动文化健康发展，在历史的长河里生生不息。 The whole world presents a splendid cultural spectacle, each culture having its own unique features and leaving a deep impression. Different cultures blend and interact with each other, enriching the forms and connotations of culture and providing spiritual nourishment for people. We should adopt an inclusive and free mindset towards every culture, promote its healthy development, and ensure its continuous existence in the long course of history
