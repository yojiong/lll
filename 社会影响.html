社会影响Social influence

通过一些媒体和社会的传播，导致种族歧视的偏见加剧。媒体或电影中常常不自觉地使用带有种族偏见的语言和画面，强化了公众对某些种族的刻板印象。 例如，在疫情期间，部分媒体使用“中国病毒”等词汇，直接导致了针对亚裔的偏见增加。媒体在报道相关事件时， 往往存在信息选择上的片面和武断性。某些媒体在报道犯罪新闻时，更倾向于强调少数族裔的犯罪行为，而忽视其他群体的类似行为，这种选择性报道进一步加深了公众偏见行为。

Social influence

The spread of racial discrimination has been exacerbated through the dissemination by some media and society. Media or films often unconsciously use language and images with racial bias, reinforcing the public's stereotypes about certain races. For instance, during the pandemic, some media used terms like "Chinese virus", directly leading to an increase in prejudice against Asians. When reporting related events, the media often show partiality and arbitrariness in information selection. Some media tend to emphasize the criminal acts of minority groups when reporting crime news, while ignoring similar behaviors of other groups. This selective reporting further deepens public prejudice. Social inequality is widespread, leading to unequal treatment of ethnic minorities in basic rights such as education, employment and medical care. In the United States, ethnic minorities often face more obstacles in accessing educational resources, employment opportunities and medical services.
