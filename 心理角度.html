心理角度Psychological perspective

多种心理机制相互交织，共同促成了这种偏见的产生。 人类在认知过程中简化信息，以快速对事件进行判断。这种认知方式很大程度上容易导致刻板印象的形成。 例如，将某个种族的少数犯罪行为泛化为该种族的普遍特征，从而对该种族形成偏见。

面对陌生的群体，人们往往会产生恐惧和不确定感。这种情绪容易导致偏见的产生，因为人们会将未知的群体视为潜在的威胁。偏见的形成还受到社会环境和文化的影响。 儿童在成长过程中，通过观察父母、同伴和媒体等渠道，学习到了对不同种族的偏见。这种模仿和学习的过程使得偏见在社会中得以代代相传种族偏见影响了少数族裔的社会认同和自我认知。 长期受到偏见和歧视的少数族裔，可能会对自己的身份产生怀疑和否定，这种心理上的压迫进一步削弱了社会的凝聚力。

Psychological perspective A variety of psychological mechanisms interweave with each other, jointly contributing to the emergence of this prejudice. Humans simplify information in the cognitive process to make quick judgments on events. This way of cognition is likely to lead to the formation of stereotypes to a large extent. For example, generalizing a few criminal acts of a certain ethnic group as the common characteristics of that ethnic group, thereby forming prejudice against that ethnic group. When facing unfamiliar groups, people often experience fear and a sense of uncertainty. This kind of emotion is prone to lead to the emergence of prejudice because people regard unknown groups as potential threats. The formation of prejudice is also influenced by the social environment and culture. During the process of growing up, children learn biases against different races by observing their parents, peers and the media. This process of imitation and learning enables prejudice to be passed down from generation to generation in society. Racial prejudice affects the social identity and self-perception of ethnic minorities. Ethnic minorities who have long been subject to prejudice and discrimination may doubt and deny their own identities. This psychological oppression further weakens the cohesion of society.
